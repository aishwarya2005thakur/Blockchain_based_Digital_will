A blockchain-based digital will built on the Aptos chain using the Move programming language enables secure, transparent, and tamper-proof management of inheritance. The will is encoded as a smart contract in Move, defining how digital or tokenized assets are distributed to beneficiaries upon predefined conditions, such as proof of death or inactivity. Leveraging Aptos’ high throughput and low latency, the system ensures fast and reliable execution, while Move’s resource-oriented design guarantees strong asset safety and prevents duplication or unauthorized access. This approach eliminates intermediaries, reduces legal friction, and provides a decentralized, verifiable mechanism for managing and executing wills.
